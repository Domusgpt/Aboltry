How to Play Abalone

  • Select 1-3 marbles in a line
  • Move them one space in any direction
  • Broadside moves: Move marbles sideways (no pushing)
  • Inline moves: Push opponent's marbles if you have more
  • 3 marbles can push 1 or 2 opponent marbles
  • 2 marbles can push 1 opponent marble
  • Push 6 opponent marbles off the board to win
